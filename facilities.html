Other Facilities

  • The place has a huge conference room. It prestigiously held the 1986 SAARC meeting. It was hosted by Indira Gandhi.
  • The VVIP dining hall accommodates 25 people. It has a luxurious interior with exquisite furniture.
  • Apart from that there is a dining hall which accommodates 25 people.

Prior booking is available and can be done online. Also, online booking is available for entrance as well as parking. There is time restriction for entry and exit. It is between 6 am - 6 pm.

 

The security offered is impressive. Policemen are available around the clock. CCTV surveillance is enabled. Continuous monitoring by departmental officials ensure greater safety and maintenance of law and order.

 

The horticulture department also runs a vegetarian restaurant atop the hill. There is a non-vegetarian restaurant called Mayura run by the Karnataka Tourism Department.

 

The hill top is connected from the foot of the hill by a well maintained road which spans 8 kilometers. Medical, postal, telegraph and telephone facilities are available on the hilltop. Well maintained public toilet facility is also available.
